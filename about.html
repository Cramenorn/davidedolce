About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Davide Dolce, I am specialized in Web and Cloud technologies. I've experience with .NET Framework, .NET Core, Microsoft Azure, C#, Microsoft Dynamics, Windows and GNU/Linux, I also developed in Typescript, Node.js, C/C++, Python, Ruby, Lua, Laravel, PHP, Java, JSP, Vue.js.

I'm working as external IT Consultant at Reply (Turin, Italy) and I am contributing to a project for a client with milion of records to manage, previously I worked at Welol Next (Turin, Italy) for 6 months as Full Stack Developer.

I got a Diploma in Computer Science from Giuseppe Peano (Italy) high school and a Diploma in Integrated Back-End Services, post-high school course from ITS ICT Piemonte (Italy) with a score of 84/100.

I participated to a project at school, the main task was to create an app for IOS and Android for lawyers and customers, my team got the fifth place, I was responsible for the BackEnd development which consisted to create APIs for the FrontEnd developers. I created two videogames, they are based on the fighting game style, Supreme Word Of Fighters has reached 2k of downloads.
